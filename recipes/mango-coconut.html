Mango Coconut Shrimp Curry

Description

Dive into the flavors of the tropics with this delightful Mango Coconut Shrimp Curry. Succulent shrimp simmered in a creamy coconut milk sauce infused with fresh mango, ginger, and aromatic spices. This dish is a perfect blend of sweet and savory, guaranteed to transport you to paradise with every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz/400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  2. Step 2: Add curry powder and turmeric powder. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Step 3: Pour in coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Step 4: Add shrimp and mango. Cook for 5-7 minutes or until shrimp is cooked through.
  5. Step 5: Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Step 6: Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
