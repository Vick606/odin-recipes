Pumpkin Pancakes Recipe

Description

Here is my favorite Pumpkin pancake recipe. Nutritious and quick pumpkin pancakes from scratch. Developed for my pumpkin-loving son. Serve warm with hot Kericho gold tea.

Ingredients

What you'll need to make these pancakes:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • White sugar - desired
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup fresh milk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Step 1: Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, and nutmeg together in a bowl.
  2. Step 2: Whisk milk, pumpkin, egg, and oil together in a bowl. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture and stir until just moistened. Batter will be slightly lumpy.
  3. Step 3: Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat to 175 degrees C. Drop batter by large spoonfuls onto the griddle and cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, 1 to 3 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Flip and cook until browned on the other side, about 1 minute more.
  5. Step 5: Repeat with remaining batter.
