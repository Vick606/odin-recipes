Seafood Delight Stew

Description

A hearty and flavorful stew packed with fresh seafood, vegetables, and herbs, perfect for a cozy dinner. This recipe combines the sweetness of fish with the robust flavors of shellfish, all simmered in a rich broth that's both satisfying and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) mixed seafood (shrimp, scallops, squid)
  • 2 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 large carrot, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Step 1: Prepare the Seafood: If using frozen seafood, thaw according to package instructions. If fresh, clean the seafood well. Shell shrimp if necessary and slice squid into rings.
  2. Step 2: Sauté Vegetables: Heat a large pot over medium heat. Add a tablespoon of olive oil. Once hot, add the onions, carrots, and potatoes. Sauté until the vegetables start to soften, about 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Add Garlic and Herbs: Stir in the minced garlic, bay leaf, and dried thyme. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Step 4: Mix in Tomatoes and Broth: Pour in the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth. Stir well to combine everything. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low.
  5. Step 5: Simmer: Cover the pot and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender.
  6. Step 6: Add Seafood: Gently fold in the seafood. Be careful not to overcook the seafood; it should remain firm and juicy. Cook for about 3-5 minutes, depending on the size of the pieces.
  7. Step 7: Season: Taste the stew and season with salt and pepper as needed.
  8. Step 8: Serve: Ladle the stew into bowls and garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.
